Returns & refunds

You have 30 days from delivery to return any item for a refund or exchange.

What we accept

  • Unused items in original, unopened packaging
  • Items showing manufacturer defects
  • Items damaged in shipping (please report within 7 days)

What we can't accept

  • Items that have been used or installed
  • Personal hygiene products (e.g. in-ear buds with tips removed) for health reasons
  • Final sale items (clearly marked at purchase)

How to start a return

  1. Email support@shopflow.io with your order number.
  2. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours.
  3. Drop the package at any carrier location.
  4. Once we receive it, your refund processes within 5–10 business days.

Refund methods

Refunds go back to the original payment method. If that's not possible (e.g. expired card), we'll issue store credit instead.

Exchanges

We can exchange for size, color, or a different model. Email us and we'll set it up — no extra shipping charge.

Privacy policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026

What we collect

When you place an order, we collect your name, email, shipping/billing address, phone number, and payment details. We also collect usage data (pages visited, items viewed) to improve your experience.

How we use it

  • Process and ship your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Improve our products and recommendations
  • Send marketing emails (only if you opted in)

What we never do

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share your data with third parties for their marketing
  • Store your full credit card number — payment processors handle that

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to delete it, or unsubscribe from marketing at any time. Email support@shopflow.io.

Cookies

We use a few cookies to remember your cart, currency, and preferences. We don't use third-party tracking cookies.

Questions about privacy? Get in touch.

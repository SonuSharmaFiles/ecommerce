Shipping

We ship most orders within 1 business day of receiving them.

Rates & speeds

Standard (domestic)3–5 business days · Free over $75, else $7.50

International7–14 business days · Calculated at checkout

Tracking

Every order comes with a tracking number you'll receive by email. You can also track your order on our order tracking page.

Where we ship

We currently ship to the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, EU, Australia, New Zealand, India, Nepal, and most of Southeast Asia. Some restrictions may apply for certain product categories due to local regulations.

Customs & duties

For international orders, duties and taxes may be charged on delivery — these are determined by the destination country and aren't included in our shipping rates.

Lost or stolen packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you can't find it, please wait 48 hours (sometimes carriers mark "delivered" early) and then contact us. We'll work with you to resolve it.

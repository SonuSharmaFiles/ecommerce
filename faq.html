Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

Orders

How do I track my order?
Visit our order tracking page and enter your order ID and email.
Can I change or cancel my order?
You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it. Contact us right away with your order number.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash on delivery for select regions.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping is 3–5 business days. Express delivery is 1–2 business days. International orders take 7–14 business days.
Is shipping free?
Yes, on standard shipping for orders over $75. Otherwise it's $7.50.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — to most countries. International rates calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Returns

What's your return policy?
30 days from delivery, free returns for unused items in original packaging. See full policy →
How do I start a return?
Email support@shopflow.io with your order ID and we'll send you a prepaid return label.
How long do refunds take?
Once we receive your return, refunds appear in your account within 5–10 business days.

Account & privacy

How do you handle my data?
We never sell your data. Read our privacy policy →
Is checkout secure?
Yes. We use SSL encryption for every page and never store full credit card numbers.
